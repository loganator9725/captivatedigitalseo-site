## Structured resource content

This template is aimed at longer operational material that benefits from Markdown authoring while still sitting inside the same validated route inventory.

## Why make it a separate page type?

The standard page layout works well for general site pages, but resource-heavy content often needs a different reading rhythm and a clearer summary surface.

## What this proves

The site generator can expand to new content surfaces without changing how routes are built, validated, optimized, and deployed.
